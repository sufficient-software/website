Menu Bar Calendar

Made by Sufficient Software

The Menu Bar Calendar is a quick-acccess agenda for MacOS that runs directly in your menu bar. Clicking on it's icon opens a small window that shows you the month's calendar, your agenda for the day, and a few tools to help you coordinate your schedule with others.

Features

Quickly see all of your events for the day, across all of your calendars

Hit the JOIN button to open video calls right from the app.

If its a google meet, always selects the right account to join from.

An availability view that helps you quickly find and copy open time slots to share with others.

Choose a time zone to report all your availability in their timezone.

See how busy you are at a glance with the heatmap view

  • Upcoming Meeting Indicator - Menu bar icon shows the number of minutes until your next meeting so you know how much time you have to wrap up what you're doing.
  • Multiple Calendar Support - Integrates directly with the MacOS calendar accounts, so you have access to all of your calendars right away. Plus, hide the ones you don't need daily quick access to.
  • Choose which app to use for video calls - If you don't like using a service's native app for video calls, you can choose to open them in your browser instead. Or if you prefer a specific browser for some services (Meet in Chrome) but others in another (Teams in Safari), you can set that up too.
  • Configure Working Days/Times - Set when you are working so you only send availability during those times.
